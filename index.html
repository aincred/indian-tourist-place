Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an iconic symbol of India. It is a white marble mausoleum located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, and was built by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and considered one of the Seven Wonders of the World.
